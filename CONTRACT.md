## 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

Developer will design, build, and deploy a custom web-based business management application to replace the Client's current QuickBooks system. The app will manage customers, employees, invoicing, and related business operations for the Client's commercial and residential electrical divisions.

## 3. WHAT'S INCLUDED (Essential + Standard)

### Essential Features
- Customer database (add, edit, manage customers)
- Employee management (up to 20 per division, commercial and residential kept separate)
- Create, send, and track professional invoices
- Payment recording and outstanding balance tracking
- Revenue reporting by division
- Mobile-friendly PWA (works on phones, installs like an app)
- Offline support (works without internet, syncs when back online)

### Standard Features (in addition to Essential)
- Full payroll engine (gross pay, taxes, overtime, net pay calculations)
- Pay stub generation
- Time tracking (clock in/out from mobile)
- Job management (create jobs, assign employees, track status)
- Estimates/quotes with one-click conversion to invoice
- Enhanced reports (payroll, job profitability, employee hours, tax summaries)
- PDF generation for invoices, pay stubs, and estimates
- QuickBooks data migration (all customers, employees, invoices, and payment history transferred to the new app)

### Premium Features (Optional Add-On — $3,300)
- AI-powered smart estimates (learns from historical jobs)
- Advanced analytics dashboard
- Tax form generation (W-2, 1099-NEC, quarterly federal and state forms)
- Subcontractor (1099) management
- Bank transaction import and email invoice sending
